Research Methods and Professional Practice

On this page you will find artefacts created throughout
the duration of the module.

Learning Objectives:

  1. Appraise the professional, legal, social, cultural and ethical issues that affect computing professionals.
  2. Appraise the principles of academic investigation, applying them to a research topic in the applicable computing field.
  3. Evaluate critically existing literature, research design and methodology for the chosen topic, including data analysis processes.
  4. Produce and evaluate critically the resulting research proposal for the chosen topic.

About the Module

This module provided me with a practical foundation in research methods that will enable me to conduct research in a practical setting.

Collaborative Discussions

Two Collaborative discussions took place during the module. A Collaborative Discussion is where all students participating in the module would post on a forum and discuss relevant topics to gain a better understanding of the topic. Discussion one was based on Codes of Ethics and Proffessional Conduct, and Discussion 2 was based on a case study exploring the accuracy of information. Photo by Dylan Gillis on Unsplash
